Talltale Tavern

Welcome to the Talltale Tavern! We are a DnD one shot podcast dedicated to bringing you the finest in fun and engaging roleplay stories every month. Adventures are served up in two courses delivered to you on the first and third Wednesday of each month. You'll laugh. You'll cry. You'll laugh so hard you'll cry!
